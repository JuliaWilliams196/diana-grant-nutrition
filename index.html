About Me

Alongside being a nutritionist, I’ve also been an Ontario Certified Teacher since 2010. I’ve taught junior kindergarten through grade 12 in Toronto, South Korea, and in China!

What is Holistic Nutrition?

Practitioners of holistic nutrition take into account the whole person when they prescribe protocols.

These protocols can include dietary, supplement, and lifestyle recommendations, and may involve botanicals (herbs).

We dive deep and strive to uncover the root cause(s) of any imbalances or dysfunctions in the body.

Common Reasons to Get Nutritional Counseling

Some of the most common complaints I hear include:

  • Fatigue, stress, and burnout

  • Digestive issues

  • Anxiety, depression, and/or mood swings

  • Difficulties with sleep

  • Joint and muscle pain

  • Hormonal imbalances

  • Skin challenges

Holistic nutrition can help with a wide variety of complex health conditions, including:

  • Autism and ADHD

  • Brain injury and concussion

  • Celiac, Crohn’s, IBS, and other digestive disorders

  • Food allergies and sensitivities

  • Addiction

  • Diabetes

  • Hair loss

  • Eating disorders

  • PMS, PCOS, and menopause

  • Enlarged prostate

  • Neurological issues such as Alzheimer’s and Parkinson’s

  • Thyroid disorders

  • Adrenal fatigue

  • Fertility, pregnancy, and postpartum support

  • Infant and childhood nutrition

  • Nutrition for seniors

  • … and more!

All consultations can take place either online, or at my office in Toronto.
